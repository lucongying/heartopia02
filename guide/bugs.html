🎯 Bug Catching Basics

Bug catching is one of the most challenging hobbies in Heartopia. Unlike fishing, you need to hold your net, sneak up on targets, and swing at the right moment. Each insect has different alertness and flight patterns.

💡 Beginner Tip: Start with the Cabbage White — slow flight, wide distribution, low price but won't flee too fast. Master the "sneak key" (gentle joystick push) before challenging rare insects.

Bug Catching Steps

  1. Equip bug net (Backpack → Tools → Bug Net)
  2. Find target insect (different species in different habitats)
  3. Hold sneak key and approach slowly (don't run!)
  4. Release to swing when in net range!
  5. Insect is automatically added to your backpack on hit
