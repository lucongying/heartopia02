🔭 Birdwatching Basics

Birdwatching is the most relaxing and healing hobby in Heartopia. No quick reactions needed — just patience and observation. Point your binoculars at birds and wait for the ID bar to fill.

💡 Beginner Tip: You start with Basic Binoculars (2x zoom). Begin with pigeons at the town square — they barely fly away. Higher birdwatching levels unlock better binoculars for distant rare birds.

How to Birdwatch

  1. Equip binoculars (Backpack → Tools → Binoculars)
  2. Spot target bird (see bird shadow or hear bird call)
  3. Raise binoculars and aim at the bird
  4. Hold steady until the ID bar fills
  5. Success! Bird record added to your encyclopedia
