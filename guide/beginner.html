🎓 Beginner's Guide

Just arrived in Heartopia? This guide will help you get started quickly

📋 Day 1 Must-Do Checklist

Here's what you should prioritize on your first day:

  • ✓Create Character — Customize face, hair, outfit. Don't worry about mistakes; you can change later for free
  • ✓Complete Tutorial — Follow prompts to learn basic controls (movement, interaction, backpack)
  • ✓Claim Starter Items — Receive fishing rod, Universal Bait x20, Basic Seeds x10
  • ✓Catch Your First Fish — Go to the nearest river with Universal Bait to practice casting and reeling
  • ✓Plant First Crops — Sow seeds in your garden, water them, and wait for growth
  • ✓Meet Your First Neighbor — Randomly encounter other players at Town Square; greet them to unlock social features
  • ✓Sell First Harvest — Sell fish/crops to the shop to earn your first gold
  • ✓Upgrade Your Home — Use your first gold to buy a furniture or decoration piece

🖥️ UI Overview

💡 Quick Overview: Top bar (gold/stamina/backpack), left joystick (movement), right buttons (interact/fishing/building), bottom hotbar (tool switching).

Interface Areas

  • Top Status Bar: Shows gold, stamina, backpack capacity. Stamina recovers by eating or sleeping
  • Bottom-Left Joystick: Controls character movement with 360° freedom
  • Bottom-Right Buttons: Interact key (interact with NPCs/items), Tool key (use equipped tool)
  • Bottom Hotbar: Quick-switch between rod/net/watering can/camera, up to 4 slots
  • Top-Right Menu: Open backpack, map, friends list, settings, encyclopedia

⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes

❌ Mistake 1: Fishing nonstop ignoring stamina. When stamina depletes, you can't use tools. Eat food every ~10 catches, or sleep at home to recover.
❌ Mistake 2: Selling all materials. Some "worthless" materials (herbs, mushrooms) are key cooking ingredients. Keep at least 5 of each in storage.
❌ Mistake 3: Ignoring social features. Social interaction is Heartopia's core charm. Visit neighbors' homes for inspiration; gifting accelerates friendship.
❌ Mistake 4: Focusing on only one hobby. The six hobbies (fishing/cooking/cats/bugs/birds/gardening) complement each other. Fish for cooking, cook for gifting, etc.
❌ Mistake 5: Not checking daily shop refresh. The town shop refreshes random items daily — limited furniture and rare seeds appear. Miss it = wait for next random appearance.

💎 Advanced Tips

  • Stamina Management: Sleeping at home recovers all stamina for free. Cooked food not only restores stamina but also provides bonus buffs.
  • Rain Strategy: Rain is prime fishing time (rare fish rate up). Also spawns special creatures like snails and frogs.
  • Season Awareness: Each season has unique fish and insects. Miss them = wait a year (or visit another player's world).
  • Backpack Expansion: Prioritize upgrading backpack with gold. Material collection is core to this game's fun.
  • Daily Login: Log in 7 consecutive days for rare items and gold rewards.
