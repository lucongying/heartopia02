🎯 Fishing Basics

Fishing is one of the first hobbies unlocked in Heartopia and the most stable way to make money. You can fish in four types of waters: rivers, lakes, ponds, and oceans. Different waters yield different fish.

💡 Beginner Tip: You start with Universal Bait x20. Try fishing for carp in the river first to master casting and reeling before challenging rare fish.

How to Fish

  1. Walk to the edge of any water body (river/lake/pond/ocean)
  2. Open backpack, equip fishing rod and bait
  3. Hold the cast button, wait for the bobber to land
  4. Tap to reel in quickly when the bobber sinks!
  5. Enter the fight phase: pull in the opposite direction of the fish

📍 Best Fishing Spots

🏆 Highest Profit: Autumn ocean salmon (420💰) + Summer night river catfish (350💰). Alternate fishing for 5000+ gold daily.
⚠️ Note: Rare fish (Arowana, Sunfish, etc.) have extremely low spawn rates. Use premium bait in the right season and allow plenty of time.
