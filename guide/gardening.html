🌱 Gardening Basics

Gardening is the most stable passive income source in Heartopia. Plant crops in your garden, water regularly, and wait for harvest. Different crops have different growth cycles, watering needs, and seasonal preferences.

💡 Beginner Recommendation: You start with Basic Seeds x10. Plant lettuce (2-day growth) and tomatoes (3-day growth) first — fast growth with low failure cost.

Gardening Steps

  1. Use hoe to till soil in your garden (creates planting plots)
  2. Select and sow seeds
  3. Water with watering can (some crops need multiple waterings)
  4. Wait for growth (check the progress bar)
  5. Harvest when ripe! Some crops can be harvested multiple times

💡 Gardening Tips

  • Season Match: In-season crops grow 20% faster. Off-season planting extends growth time.
  • Repeat Harvest: Tomatoes, blueberries and similar crops can fruit again without replanting (up to 3 times).
  • Watering Trick: Rain automatically waters crops — no manual watering needed. Check weather forecasts!
  • Fertilizer: Fertilizer reduces growth time by 20%. Highly recommended for high-value crops (watermelon, pumpkin).
  • Garden Layout: Same-type plants adjacent trigger the "Companion" effect, increasing yield by 10%.
🏆 Highest Profit: Watermelon (600💰/each) + Pumpkin (300💰/each). Long growth cycle but best daily profit per plot.
