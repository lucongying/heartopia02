🔨 Building System Basics

Building and decorating is the most creative system in Heartopia. Freely place furniture, change wall colors, arrange garden decor — create your one-of-a-kind dream home. Furniture comes from shops, carpentry, event rewards and rare drops.

💡 Beginner Tip: Your starter home includes basic furniture (single bed, kitchen counter, basic table and chairs). Prioritize buying a Wardrobe and Bookshelf — increasing your home score unlocks more furniture slots.

Furniture Sources

  • Shop Purchase: Most direct way. Shop inventory refreshes daily — buy what you like quickly!
  • Carpentry Crafting: Collect wood/stone/metal and craft at the workshop
  • Event Exclusive: Seasonal and collab event furniture — gone when the event ends
  • Rare Drops: Extremely low chance to get special decor while fishing/catching bugs/birdwatching
⚠️ Warning: Event-exclusive furniture (MLP Rainbow Sofa, Sakura Tree Lamp) cannot be traded. Miss the event = wait for a rerun!
