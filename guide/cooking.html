🥘 Cooking System Basics

Cooking is a core hobby in Heartopia that not only restores stamina but also provides temporary buffs (speed boost, warmth, social affinity).

🔓 Unlock: After completing the tutorial, place a Kitchen Counter in your home to unlock the cooking system. Start with 5 basic recipes.

Cooking Steps

  1. Approach kitchen counter and press "Cook"
  2. Select a recipe to make
  3. Confirm you have all ingredients (missing items are highlighted)
  4. Press "Start Cooking" and wait for completion
  5. The dish is automatically stored in your backpack. Eat, sell, or gift it!

💡 Cooking Leveling Strategy

  • Level 1-2: Repeatedly make "Grilled Fish" and "Garden Salad" — ingredients are easy to get, low cost
  • Level 3-5: Make "Fish Soup" and "Fried Rice" — good value, decent XP
  • Level 6-8: Make "Sashimi Platter" and "Ramen" — generous XP
  • Level 9-10: Challenge the "Golden Feast" — ultimate dish worth showing off
