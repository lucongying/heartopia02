💝 Friendship System

Building relationships with NPCs is the core of Heartopia's social system. Increase friendship through gifting, chatting, and completing quests to unlock exclusive recipes, discounts, storylines and hidden features.

💡 Gifting Rules: Each NPC accepts one gift per day. ❤️ Loved = +3 FP, 👍 Liked = +1, 👎 Hated = -2. Gifting on their birthday doubles the effect!
