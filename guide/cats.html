🐾 Getting Started with Cats

In Heartopia, having a cat is the warmest and most healing hobby. You can meet stray cats around town, build trust through feeding and interaction, then adopt them. Each cat has unique personality, food preferences, and behavior patterns.

💡 How to get your first cat: After completing the tutorial, you'll meet a little white cat at the Town Square. Feed it fish for 3 consecutive days to take it home! This is the only cat obtained through the main quest.

Adoption Process

  1. Explore town and spot stray cats (different breeds in different locations)
  2. Feed its favorite food daily to increase affection
  3. When affection is maxed, the "Adopt" option appears
  4. Place a cat tree and cat bed at your home
  5. Happily take them home!
⚠️ Note: Each cat can only be fed once per day. Feeding disliked food won't increase affection; feeding hated food may even decrease it!
